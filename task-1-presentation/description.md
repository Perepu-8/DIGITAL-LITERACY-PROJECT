# Digital Literacy & Professional Presence

[cite_start]This guide defines **Digital Literacy** as the ability to safely and responsibly access, evaluate, and communicate information using technology[cite: 3, 4, 5].

### 🛠️ Key Student Tools
* [cite_start]**Learning & STEM**: Khan Academy, BYJU'S, Coursera, GeoGebra, and Wolfram Alpha[cite: 7, 13].
* [cite_start]**Productivity**: Notion, OneNote, Google Docs, and Trello[cite: 8, 10].
* [cite_start]**Creativity**: Canva and Prezi[cite: 14].

### 🛡️ Safety & Professionalism
* [cite_start]**Best Practices**: Use antivirus/firewalls, log out of shared devices, and backup data[cite: 19, 21, 23].
* [cite_start]**Online Presence**: Build credibility through a clean profile, clear bio, and active engagement on LinkedIn and GitHub[cite: 28].
