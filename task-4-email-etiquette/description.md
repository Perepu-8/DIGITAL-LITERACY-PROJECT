## Task 4: Email & Digital Communication Etiquette

In this task, I practised professional communication by drafting two formal emails and creating a social media etiquette checklist. The emails included a request for an assignment extension and an inquiry for a summer internship, both following proper structure, tone, and formatting.

I also created a checklist of Do’s and Don’ts for responsible social media use, which highlights the importance of maintaining a positive and professional digital presence.

One situation where poor digital communication caused a problem is when a student sends an informal or unclear email to a professor, leading to misunderstanding or being ignored. This can be avoided by using a clear subject line, proper greeting, and respectful tone.

Overall, this task helped me understand the importance of clarity, professionalism, and responsibility in digital communication.
