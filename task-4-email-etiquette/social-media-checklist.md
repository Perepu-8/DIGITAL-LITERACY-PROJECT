# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist

## ✅ Do’s
- Think before you post – once it’s online, it’s permanent.
- Use strong privacy settings to control who can see your content.
- Share only verified and accurate information.
- Maintain a respectful and professional tone.
- Give credit to original creators when sharing their content.

## ❌ Don’ts
- Don’t share personal or sensitive information (address, phone number, etc.).
- Don’t spread fake news or unverified information.
- Don’t engage in online fights or abusive behavior.
- Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content.
- Don’t overshare your location or daily activities.
