## Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness & Prevention

In this task, I explored cybercrime by creating a case study and a prevention checklist. I chose a common type of cybercrime and explained how it occurs, who is targeted, and what consequences victims may face. This helped me understand how easily people can be tricked through online scams and fraudulent activities.

I also created a ‘Stay Safe Online’ checklist with practical tips, including precautions for UPI and online payments. Additionally, I included official reporting channels such as the National Cyber Crime Portal and helpline 1930.

One surprising insight was how convincing and well-planned many cyber scams are, making them difficult to identify. As a result, I will be more cautious while sharing personal information and making online transactions, ensuring better digital safety in the future.
